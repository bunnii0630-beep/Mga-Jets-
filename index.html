F (Fighter)

Fighter aircraft are designed for air combat. Examples include the F-22 Raptor and F-35 Lightning II.

Examples: F-22, F-35

B (Bomber)

Bombers are used to drop bombs on targets. Examples: B-2 Spirit, B-52 Stratofortress.
